Generation IV note on molten salt reactors

Molten salt reactor concepts are often discussed in the context of Generation IV
systems. Analysts usually encounter the terms molten salt reactor, process heat,
high-temperature applications, low-pressure operation, materials compatibility
and fuel cycle flexibility.

For thematic classification this kind of document should be associated with
advanced reactors, Generation IV, thermal efficiency and industrial heat rather
than with conventional pressurized water reactor maintenance.
